Hi Moksha,

I understand if you'd rather not stay in touch. I've been thinking that maybe things felt off after we met, or that you've had a lot going on. Either way, I'm really sorry for pushing you to talk and for coming across as anxious. I should have respected your space more.

I also want to apologise for not being completely honest earlier. I think I was just scared of coming across as weak.

✦

I know what it feels like to lose someone who really matters — it affected me more than I expected. I spiraled into self-hate, isolated myself, and pushed people away. There were days I genuinely didn't see a reason to keep going, and at one point, I even tried to end things.

Things at home didn't help either. I often felt misunderstood, constantly judged, and alone in a way that's hard to explain. The environment around me wasn't exactly supportive, and I picked up habits like smoking weed daily just to feel something calmer — something numb.

✦

But then you came into my life.

Something about you felt different from the start. Talking to you, waiting for your texts — it made me feel alive again. For the first time in a long while, I actually wanted to do better. I didn't feel the urge to escape anymore. I wanted to work on myself.

I'm not saying everything is suddenly perfect, but you've had a real impact on me, and I felt like you deserved to know the truth.

✦

And then I met you.

The first time I saw you, I couldn't stop looking at your eyes. I wasn't even planning to give you my jacket, but I just got lost in that moment. Even while riding, I kept adjusting my mirrors just to catch another glimpse of you.

What happened later that evening… it wasn't planned, and I'm really sorry if I crossed a line with the way I kissed you. I should've handled that better.

But beyond that, I genuinely liked everything about you — your voice, your smile, the way you roll your eyes… all of it. For the first time in a long while, I actually wanted to show up for someone. To do things right. To be someone who could make you smile and feel taken care of.

And when you told me about your past, it hit me in a way I didn't expect. I could relate more than I said at the time, and it made me feel like we understood each other on a deeper level.

✦

I never imagined I'd feel like this about someone. I wanted to do things right — take you out on fun dates, get you flowers, pick you up and drop you, open doors for you… just show you that I can care, and that you deserve that kind of softness and respect.

Out of so many people in this world, we somehow crossed paths — and that means something to me.

Maybe I messed things up, or maybe the timing just wasn't right. Either way, I'd really like another chance — to take you out, spend time with you, and let you see who I actually am.

✦

Not to rush into anything or force a relationship. I just want to know you better, and let you know me too. Simple as that.

I've spent a lot of my life letting go of things I wanted without even trying. I'm not doing that this time. I'd rather try and be told no than sit with a "what if" later.

I meant what I said — I can handle you, your moods, your chaos, all of it. And I'd never treat you the way you were treated before.

I'd really like to start fresh — no pressure, just taking things at our own pace, if you're open to it.

Maybe we could meet for something simple, like a clay workshop I've been wanting to try. I think it'd be fun, and I'd love to do that with you.

With all sincerity,
Someone who means it
